About Us

Crunchies Fried Chicken is a first-grade quick service restaurant that offers a variety of quality fast food products and first-class services. We are currently spread across the South-South, South-East and South-West regions of Nigeria, with over 20 years of experience. We have carved the niche of excellence in the fast food market with a service per excellence acquired from interacting with our valued customers.
